Our Mission

Outpost Gray provides a unique approach in a changing world. We are an international firm specializing in advisory, training, intelligence and direct action capabilities for clients in support of security and freedom throughout the world. Our highly experienced team of operators and seasoned professionals provide creative solutions to challenges in unstable environments while serving as a force multiplier on behalf of clients.

About Us

What We Do

Outpost Gray teams are composed of highly experienced men and women from the Special Operations community, specialized law enforcement/SWAT professionals and elite security specialists. All have been challenged in some of the world’s largest conflicts in efforts to defeat terrorism, the drug trade and human trafficking.

Outpost Gray teams are highly trained with access to systems, networks and resources to increase the winning capabilities of clients in pursuit of peace, stability and freedom.

We understand that the current political climate in the world is driving the need more than ever to develop international partnerships. Outpost Gray takes a unique approach with our clients which makes us more than ‘just another contracting company’.
